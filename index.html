    Vind + Vågor

    A friendly, ethical cosmetic company based in Gothenburg, Sweden.

    About us

    20 years of creating natural + ethical products

    Established in 1998 by sisters Elsa + Maja Sjöberg, Vind + Vågor creates skin, hair and body care products made from only the finest natural ingredients. Our philosophy has always been to keep things simple, clean and ethical.

    Our products

    Skin, hair, body and home.

    From creams and cleansers to shampoos and scrubs. All of our products are created with the very highest degree of care and craftsmanship. Our team of scientists thoroughly test each and every product to make sure that they are suitable for even the most sensitive skin.
